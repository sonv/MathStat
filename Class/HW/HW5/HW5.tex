    \begin{equation*}
        f(x) = 
        \begin{cases}
            0 \,, & x^2 < y < 2x^2 \\

        \end{cases}
    \end{equation*}
    Show that $f_x(0,0)$ and $f_y(0,0)$ exist, but $f$ is not differentiable at
